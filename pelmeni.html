The fried pelmeni recipe

To make gread pelmeni you need:

  • Frozen pelmeni
  • Cooking oil
  • A bit of water
  • Some ketchup (optional)

Directions:

  1. Add frozen pelmeni to pan with a generous amount of oil
  2. Fry with lid on pan first, then remove the lid and fry on medium high heat until pelmeni start to get golden brown
  3. Add about 1/4 glass of water and 1/4 glass of ketchup to the pan
  4. Fry on medium heat until the pelmeni are no longer floating in liquid
  5. Enjoy with some sour cream!
