The thin and crusty pizza dough

To make a nice pizza base you need:

  • 152 g water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of active dry yeast or instant yeast
  • 240 g wheat flour
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder/soda
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:

  1. Mix water with yeast
  2. Add the flour
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Cut the mass in half, make a ball with flour around it
  5. Put the balls on floured surface and leave for 30 minutes
  6. Roll the dough into pizza shape
  7. Add toppings and bake for 8 minutes in 230 degrees Celsius
Enjoy the really tasty pizza!
