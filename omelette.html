The airfryer omelette

To make airfryer omelette you need:

  • Parchment paper or silicone mould for your airfryer
  • 3-4 eggs
  • About half a glass of milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: bacon, cherry tomatoes, cheese

Directions:

  1. Whisk eggs with milk and salt
  2. Pour the egg and milk mixture in the silicone mould or insert parchment paper in the baking tray (make sure the paper doesn't break!) and pour the mixture in the parchment paper
  3. Optionally - add cheese, bacon or cherry tomatoes to the mixture
  4. Airfry at 190 degrees Celsius for 10-15 minutes to your liking of the
Enjoy!
